TerpPark Alarm Design Project

These user interfaces were developed by myself and a group of two others when I took a User Interface class. Our goal was to design an app that made it easier for students to find parking spots on campus. We worked alongside the Department of Transportation at UMD, interviewed students to assess the user needs, and followed the standard design thinking double diamond model of design.
